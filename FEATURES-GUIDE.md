# Quick Features Guide

## 🎵 Where to Find Everything

### In the Player Bar (Bottom of Screen)

From left to right, you now have:

1. **Track Info** - Album art, title, artist
2. **Playback Controls** - Previous, Play/Pause, Next
3. **Progress Bar** - Seek through track
4. **Audio Controls**:
   - 🔊 Volume
   - 🔀 Shuffle
   - 🔁 Repeat
   - **✨ FX Button (NEW!)** - Audio Effects Panel
   - **📊 Visualizer Button (NEW!)** - Full-screen visualization
   - **🎛️ EQ Button** - 10-Band Equalizer
   - 📋 Playlist

## 🎨 Three Audio Panels

### 1. FX Panel (Left Side) - Click ✨ Magic Wand
**FxSound-Style Audio Enhancement**
- Bass Boost
- Clarity
- Ambience  
- Dynamic Boost
- Surround Sound
- Fidelity

**6 Presets**: Off, Music, Cinema, Gaming, Voice, Bass++

### 2. Visualizer (Full Screen) - Click 📊 Chart Icon
**4 Visualization Modes**
- Bars (frequency spectrum)
- Wave (waveform)
- Circular (360° spectrum)
- Particles (animated effects)

### 3. Equalizer (Right Side) - Click 🎛️ Sliders Icon
**10-Band Parametric EQ**
- 32 Hz to 16 kHz
- ±12 dB per band
- 8 Presets

## 🎯 Quick Start

1. **Play a song** from your playlist libraries
2. **Click the magic wand** (✨) for instant audio enhancement
3. **Try the "Music" preset** - balanced enhancement
4. **Click the chart icon** (📊) to see visualizations
5. **Switch between visualization modes** using the buttons at the top

## 💡 Tips

- Effects work in real-time - adjust while playing
- Combine FX + EQ for maximum control
- Visualizer reacts to the music frequency spectrum
- All settings are professional-grade Web Audio API

Enjoy your enhanced audio experience! 🎵
